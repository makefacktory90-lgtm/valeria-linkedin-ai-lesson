Skills and layers

Что ставим / создаем в Codex

Здесь “skills” — это рабочие слои машины. Часть уже есть как внешний GitHub skill, часть мы создаем как Val-specific prompts / project instructions внутри ее Codex-папки.

Layer Tool / skill What it does Status
Topic Radar Val-specific prompt Finds fresh post topics from pillars, people, market signals and recurring L&D / transformation tensions. Prepare in lesson 3.
Search Exa Finds sources, experts, reports, examples, field language and weak signals. Already introduced.
Deep Research Val Research Skill + Deep Research Turns one topic into a structured brief: sources, tensions, evidence, risks and angles. green adapted.
Pillar Router Val-specific prompt Routes each topic into Making Change Work, Making Complexity Usable or Creative Strategy for Change. red needs Val's strategic OK.
Strategy Check Val-specific prompt Checks positioning, audience, business relevance, learning mechanism and proof potential. red needs Val's OK.
Voice Rewriter Needs Val's final Tone of Voice Rewrites draft into Val's strategic challenger voice using examples and banned phrases. red needs weekend input.
Humanizer Adapted from humanize layer Adds lived observation, natural rhythm, field language and human reasoning. green ready.
De-AI Cleaner Adapted from De-AI layer Removes generic LinkedIn structure, fake drama, guru cadence and corporate fluff. green ready.
Do-ification Val-specific prompt Turns insight into a useful action: diagnostic question, design decision, checklist or next step. green ready.
Draft Library Google Drive / GitHub / local Codex folder Stores research briefs, hooks, drafts, edits, source notes and publishing status. Choose during setup.

Pillar routing

Как машина работает по разным pillars

Один и тот же workflow должен давать разные outputs по разным territories. Иначе опять получим универсальную AI-мясорубку, а нам нужна нормальная профессиональная машина.

Pillar Search for Best outputs Quality check
Making Change Work Post-launch adoption friction, workarounds, manager enablement, behavior change, rollout failures. Field note, case-style post, diagnostic checklist, manager support framework. Does this show what learning teams need to understand about real change behavior?
Making Complexity Usable Performance support, job aids, decision trees, workflow prompts, complexity-to-action examples. Framework, checklist, before/after simplification, practical design principle. Does this turn complexity into action without dumbing it down?
Creative Strategy for Change Positioning, internal comms, product launch thinking, Big Idea, message hierarchy, relevance. Contrarian opinion, rollout narrative, positioning breakdown, creative frame. Does this connect creative strategy back to learning and adoption?
