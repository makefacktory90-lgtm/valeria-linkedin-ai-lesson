Strategic positioning V2

Learning as the engine, transformation as the context

This version keeps Val inside the learning market while giving her a stronger business and transformation edge.

Positioning statement V2

Val is a business-minded learning strategist who helps organizations use learning to turn complex change into practical ways of working people can actually adopt.

For whom

Transformation leaders, L&D leaders, HR and business teams trying to make complex change stick after launch.

Against what

Training-as-content, launch-only change, generic communications, learning theater, completion metrics without behavior change.

Mechanism

Learning strategy, performance support, consulting thinking, marketing strategy and transformation experience translated into usable adoption systems.

Voice V2

Commercially sharp, evidence-aware, practical

Голос не должен уходить в corporate fluff и не должен становиться агрессивным creator voice. Рабочая зона: strategic challenger. Взрослая резкость, умная простота, бизнес-язык, доказательность и конкретные действия.

Voice rule What it means How it sounds What to avoid
Commercially sharp Every idea connects to business consequence, adoption, performance, risk or execution. “Training completion is not proof that work changed.” Nice L&D reflection with no business implication.
Evidence-aware Claims are grounded in observed patterns, research, examples or operational logic. “The workaround is data. It shows where the designed process meets real work.” Big claims that sound confident but unsupported.
Practical Posts should end with a design decision, diagnostic question, checklist or better alternative. “Before adding another module, decide what belongs in training and what belongs in the workflow.” Empty inspiration, motivational endings, vague “we need to do better”.
Slightly contrarian Challenge default assumptions without turning the post into a performance of rebellion. “People do not always resist change. Often, they resist unclear work.” Anti-corporate irritation as the whole personality.
Smart simplicity Explain complex things cleanly, without dumbing them down. No corporate буль-бульк. “More information is not more clarity. Clarity helps people act.” Jargon, overexplaining, academic fog, AI-looking structure.

Content pillars V2

Те же три территории, но learning теперь явно внутри

Пиллары не надо выбрасывать. Они стали сильнее после правки Леры: теперь каждый показывает, почему именно learning strategist нужен в messy middle of transformation.

01 · Making Change Work

Что change реально делает с людьми и командами после launch: overwhelm, workarounds, uneven manager support, old habits, different team realities.

Learning mechanism: learning teams need this human reality to design support that helps people adopt new ways of working, not just understand what changed.

Audience should remember: Val understands what really happens when change meets people and uses those insights to design learning that works.

02 · Making Complexity Usable

Про “умную простоту”: turning systems, rules, exceptions, ownership and dependencies into action without dumbing things down.

Learning mechanism: learning and performance support help people move from “I understand what changed” to “I know what I need to do differently in real work.”

Audience should remember: Val helps people navigate complexity and turn it into practical action.

03 · Creative Strategy for Change

Change needs positioning, story, Big Idea and message people can hold on to. Not just new system, new process, new dates.

Learning mechanism: learning is not only information transfer. It is an experience that helps people understand why change matters and what they need to do differently.

Audience should remember: Val brings marketing thinking into learning and transformation.

Reference voices V2

Что забираем из ориентиров

Это не список для копирования. Это механики, которые можно адаптировать под Val: clarity, evidence, business translation, smart simplicity and controlled edge.

Ориентир Что у него / нее сильное Что Val может адаптировать Что не копировать
Josh Bersin Industry analyst energy: macro trends, research, HR/L&D systems, business context. Use research-backed synthesis: “what this means for L&D teams in transformation.” Do not become broad HR analyst. Val needs narrower, more applied field voice.
Павел Безручко Без воды о менеджменте: сложные управленческие вещи простым, ясным языком. Smart simplicity: short business logic, clean definitions, usable management language. Do not copy Russian management cadence directly into U.S. LinkedIn.
David James Business-first L&D: outcomes, performance, problems before solutions. Refuse order-taking. Start with business problem and behavior change. Do not sound like vendor-polished L&D platform voice.
April Dunford Positioning discipline: audience, alternatives, value, proof, market frame. Make every post clear about what alternative thinking it replaces. Do not turn LinkedIn into positioning lessons unrelated to learning.
Szilvia Olah Sharp anti-theater pattern naming. She quickly names what feels false in HR/L&D. Borrow the courage to name learning theater and weak assumptions directly. Use carefully. Do not become grievance-driven, anti-HR or performatively harsh.

6-month LinkedIn visibility plan

Гипотеза на 6 месяцев с учетом новых вводных: не просто быть видимой как эксперт по transformation, а стать узнаваемой как learning strategist, который умеет превращать complex change в adopted ways of working.

Profile V2, voice rules, first 6 posts from three pillars. Профиль сразу считывается как learning strategist for adoption, not change consultant.

Рабочая система в Codex, hook bank, draft reviewer, weekly search routine. Val может регулярно получать не generic drafts, а черновики, близкие к ее голосу.

Открыть машину

1 · Knowledge base

Загрузить V2 positioning, updated pillars, voice rules, reference mechanics, examples of Val / not Val and the first published drafts.

2 · Search layer

Exa + Deep Research ищут sources, people, reports, field language, tensions and examples по одному pillar или post topic.

3 · Strategy check

Каждый topic проверяется: pillar fit, learning mechanism, audience relevance, business consequence, proof potential.

4 · Voice classifier

Codex сравнивает draft с voice library и помечает: too nice, too harsh, too generic, too AI, enough Val.

5 · Humanizer + de-AI

Убирает generic LinkedIn structure, fake drama, guru cadence, corporate fluff and over-clean AI transitions.

6 · Do-ification

Превращает мысль в действие: diagnostic question, design decision, checklist, better alternative or field note.
