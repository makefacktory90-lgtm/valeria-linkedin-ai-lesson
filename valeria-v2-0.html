Positioning V2.0

Learning as the mechanism, transformation as the context

Главная поправка: learning должен быть не фоном, а механизмом. Мы говорим не просто про change, а про то, как learning strategy, performance support and adoption systems помогают людям реально работать по-новому.

Updated 10% message

Val helps organizations use learning to turn complex change into new ways of working people actually adopt.

Positioning statement

Val is a business-minded learning strategist who helps organizations use learning to turn complex change into practical ways of working people can actually adopt.

Territory

Learning for the messy middle of transformation. Not learning as content production, but learning as adoption support for real work.

Final tone of voice V2.0

Strategic challenger with smart simplicity

Голос должен быть corporate-safe, но не nice ради nice. Он должен звучать быстро, ясно, бизнес-ориентированно и без corporate fluff. Резкость идет не из агрессии, а из точности.

Voice What it means How it sounds Guardrail
Commercially sharp Каждая мысль связана с business consequence, adoption, performance or execution. Training completion is not proof that work changed. Не писать мягкие L&D-размышления без ставки для бизнеса.
Evidence-aware Опора на observed patterns, research, field notes, credible sources. The workaround is not noise. It is adoption data. Не делать уверенные claims без проверки.
Practical Текст должен приводить к действию, решению, диагностике или better alternative. Before building another course, decide what belongs in training and what belongs in the workflow. Без empty inspiration и “we need to do better”.
Slightly contrarian Можно challenging assumptions, learning theater, order-taking and fake clarity. People do not always resist change. Often, they resist unclear work. Не становиться anti-HR, anti-corporate or grievance-driven.
Smart simplicity Сложные enterprise-вещи объясняются чисто, просто, без упрощенчества. More information is not more clarity. Clarity helps people act. Никакого corporate буль-бульк, jargon fog и AI-perfect cadence.

Content pillars V2.0

Три территории, каждая возвращена к learning

Пиллары оставляем, но усиливаем learning angle. Каждый пост должен отвечать: что здесь важно для learning teams, learning strategy, performance support or adoption?

01 · Making Change Work

Human reality of change after launch: overwhelm, workarounds, uneven manager support, old habits and adoption friction.

Learning mechanism: learning teams use this reality to design support that helps people adopt new ways of working, not just understand what changed.

02 · Making Complexity Usable

Smart simplicity: turning systems, rules, exceptions, ownership and dependencies into usable action.

Learning mechanism: learning and performance support help people move from “I understand what changed” to “I know what I need to do differently.”

03 · Creative Strategy for Change

Positioning, story, Big Idea and message people can hold on to, instead of disconnected updates, features and dates.

Learning mechanism: learning is not only information transfer; it helps people understand why change matters and what to do differently.

What worked

The 20-topic backlog gave enough direction: go-live, unclear work, workarounds, managers, complexity, job aids, positioning, relevance and Big Idea.

Next upgrade

Turn topic discovery into a repeatable weekly radar: trend -> pillar fit -> learning mechanism -> post angle -> draft.

Pillar Topics that already work How the machine should use them
Making Change Work Go-live is not the finish line; people resist unclear work; quiet workarounds; managers as adoption layer. Look for field examples, adoption friction, manager enablement and post-launch support patterns.
Making Complexity Usable More information is not clarity; training is not where all complexity should go; job aids; decision trees. Look for complexity-to-action cases, workflow support, performance support and smart simplicity.
Creative Strategy for Change Transformation needs positioning; internal change as product launch; features do not create adoption; Big Idea. Look for positioning, message hierarchy, relevance, rollout narrative and audience belief shifts.

Do not write the LinkedIn post yet.
First build a research brief.

Return:
1. Topic fit: pillar, learning mechanism, audience, business problem.
2. Source map: 5-8 credible sources or examples.
3. Reference voice lenses: what each reference voice would notice.
4. Tensions and contrarian angles.
5. Learning mechanism: training, performance support, workflow, managers, communication.

7. Risk check: claims, numbers, risky wording, evidence strength.
8. Post opportunities: 5 angles with hook, argument, mechanism, takeaway, format.
9. Voice guardrails: sounds like Val / generic / too nice / too harsh / too AI.
10. Exa search queries: 10 queries for better sources, people, examples or reports.

Monday flow

Что строим за час в понедельник

Если V2.0 окей, следующий шаг — не еще раз обсуждать стратегию, а собрать рабочий механизм в Codex.

Layer Purpose Output
Search layer Exa / Deep Research собирают источники, field language, tensions, examples and weak signals. Input for the agent.
Strategy layer Каждая тема сверяется с positioning, three pillars и six-month visibility goal. Strategic go / no-go.
Voice layer Voice filter проверяет, звучит ли текст как Val: disciplined contrarian, practical, evidence-aware. Voice-correct direction.
Hook layer Подбираем хуки под три пиллара, но не превращаем текст в engagement bait. Hook options.
Humanizer + De-AI Ставим как редакторские фильтры без отдельного согласования. Cleaner professional version.
Final layer Агент предлагает пост, carousel angle или featured asset. Val makes the final decision.
